A few facts about me

I've lived in Maryland my whole life, and as such love the MD flag, blue crabs, and of course -- old bay!

Some of my absolute favorite things to do in my free time include spending time in the great outdoors. I love hiking, biking, kayaking, and especially backpacking.

Favorite sports team? I disowned the Redskins a long time ago, so I'll have to go with the Nats!

Favorite sitcom? Glad you asked! How does the show about nothing sound? I know, I know. Why would an 18-year-old like myself enjoy the same show as his parents? It's just a beautifully written show. Favorite episodes? The Fusilli Jerry, The Shower Head, The Marine Biologist.

Favorite foods? Pizza, lasagna, sushi. This is a difficult to answer since there's not much food I don't love!

I could use a good haircut right now! The COVID quarantine has left me with a veritable mane of hair that fails to respond to conventional warfare.

My family has a pet bunny - named Mickey - who we've had since 2010. For those keeping track, he was fully grown when we got him, so he's at least 11 now. Bunnies are only supposed to live around 8 years!
